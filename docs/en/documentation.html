How to use Sealfie?
Once registered in our systems, which is done by contacting us

I need to validate a document linked to a transaction request.

I start sealfie, choose document

I take a photo of the document, a selfie and add a message (option)

I confirm with the biometric of my smartphone

The validation request is processed, we add additional certificates if available. The other members of the group receive notifications.

Another person receive a validation request.

She checks the photos and consistency of information.

It validates and adds a selfie.

She confirms with the biometric of her smartphone

The applicant may verify that the right people have approved the request. Contact us for technical details and computer automation.
